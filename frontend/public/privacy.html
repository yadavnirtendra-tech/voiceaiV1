Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

At OpenCalendar, we take your privacy and the security of your calendar data extremely seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our synchronization services, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Calendar Data: We access your calendar events (titles, times, locations) via Google and Microsoft APIs solely to synchronize them across your accounts.
  • OAuth Tokens: We store encrypted access and refresh tokens to maintain your connection to calendar providers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Synchronize events between your connected calendars.
  • Create "Shadow Blocks" to protect your availability.
  • Provide you with a dashboard of your upcoming meetings.

We NEVER sell your data to third parties. We do not use your calendar content for advertising or any purpose other than synchronization.

3. Data Protection

All sensitive data, including OAuth tokens, is stored using AES-256-GCM encryption. Our connections to Google and Microsoft APIs are secured via industry-standard TLS encryption.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We only store calendar event data as long as necessary to facilitate synchronization. If you disconnect an account or delete your CalendarSync AI account, we immediately delete all associated tokens and cached event data from our servers.

5. Google API Limited Use

OpenCalendar's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@opencalender.site.
