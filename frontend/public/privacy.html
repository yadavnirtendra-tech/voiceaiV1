Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

At OpenCalendar, we take your privacy and the security of your calendar data extremely seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information using state-of-the-art security measures.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our synchronization engine, we collect minimal data:

  • Account Details: Basic identifier information such as name and email.
  • Calendar Telemetry: Event timing and metadata required for synchronization.
  • OAuth Credentials: Encrypted access tokens stored securely.

2. Data Utilization

Your data remains yours. We use it solely to:

  • Establish bidirectional synchronization.
  • Calculate shadow block requirements to prevent double-booking.
  • Power your unified dashboard experience.

3. Enterprise-Grade Security

All stored credentials utilize AES-256-GCM encryption. API transactions occur exclusively over TLS 1.3 encrypted tunnels.

4. Google API Limited Use

OpenCalendar's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Questions? Contact our security team at support@opencalender.site
